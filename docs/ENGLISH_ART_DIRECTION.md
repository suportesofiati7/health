# English art direction

English and Brazilian Portuguese share one visual authority; there is no English-only correction stylesheet. Art direction is expressed by semantic HTML and the shared design system.

Authoritative guidance:

- [Visual identity](design-system/visual-identity.md)
- [Art direction](design-system/art-direction.md)
- [Editorial principles](design-system/editorial-principles.md)
- [Page families](design-system/page-families.md)
- [Typography](design-system/typography-system.md)
- [Grid and containers](design-system/grid-and-containers.md)

Each section declares `data-pattern` and `data-tone`; optional density/media data expresses deliberate variation. Source order, JavaScript and language do not choose colours or composition. English remains the structural source of truth, while layouts accommodate Brazilian Portuguese expansion without a smaller parallel design.
